What We Do

A complete revenue
intelligence platform

From enforcement and collection to capital market access — Bekkr provides the full stack of fiscal infrastructure for subnational governments.

Revenue Enforcement

Systematic identification, verification, and enforcement of revenue obligations across all taxpayer segments — from property rates to business levies.

Digital Collection

Multi-channel payment infrastructure that meets taxpayers where they are — mobile money, bank transfer, USSD, and physical touchpoints in one unified platform.

Revenue Analytics

Real-time dashboards and predictive analytics that give finance officers complete visibility into revenue streams, compliance rates, and collection forecasts.

Capital Market Access

We package subnational revenue streams into bankable instruments, connecting local governments to institutional investors and development finance institutions.

Compliance Management

Automated compliance workflows reduce manual overhead and improve taxpayer relations — with built-in appeals handling, amnesty programmes, and legal escalation.

Fiscal Advisory

Dedicated advisory support to build long-term revenue capacity — including staff training, policy design, and intergovernmental fiscal transfer optimisation.

The Platform

Revenue intelligence from data to disbursement

01

Data Integration & Assessment

We ingest all available data sources — land registries, business licences, utility connections, satellite imagery — to build a complete taxpayer register.

02

Valuation & Billing

AI-assisted valuation models generate accurate and defensible assessments. Digital bills are delivered across all channels automatically.

03

Collection & Reconciliation

Payments are collected, reconciled, and reported in real time. All revenue flows are auditable and transparent to relevant oversight bodies.

04

Capital Structuring

Proven revenue streams are structured as bankable instruments and presented to our network of institutional capital partners for financing.

Revenue collection for the world's most dynamic urban economies

Africa's cities and municipalities are growing faster than anywhere on earth — yet most collect less than 30% of the revenue they are legally owed. Bekkr changes that, starting in Ghana and expanding across the continent and global south.

Our presence on the ground, combined with enterprise-grade technology and deep fiscal policy expertise, means we deliver results where others cannot.

Capital Markets

Connecting African governments to global capital

Subnational governments have historically been shut out of capital markets. Bekkr packages proven revenue streams into bankable instruments that institutional investors can assess and fund — unlocking financing for infrastructure, services, and growth.

Revenue Securitisation

We structure future revenue flows as securities, enabling upfront capital for government priorities.

Municipal Bond Issuance

First-of-kind subnational bond programmes, structured for African credit environments and international ESG standards.

DFI & Impact Finance

Direct partnerships with development finance institutions to channel concessional finance to our government partners.

Credit Enhancement

We work with guarantors and first-loss facilities to improve the credit profile of subnational borrowings.
